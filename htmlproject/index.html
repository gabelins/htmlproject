Welcome to Creek Boathouse

Welcome to the Boathouse, where adventure meets relaxation. Our premier facility offers kayak and paddleboard rentals, a cafe serving delicious food and drinks, and a fitness program to keep you in shape. Whether you're an experienced paddler or a beginner, you'll find everything you need for an unforgettable experience.

Our top-notch equipment and knowledgeable staff will ensure you have a safe and enjoyable time on the water. Afterwards, enjoy a meal and a refreshment in our cafe, surrounded by the stunning natural beauty of our location. And if you're looking for a challenge, join our fitness program and stay active through rowing workouts, even on land.

So come on down and make memories that will last a lifetime. We can't wait to see you at the Boathouse!
